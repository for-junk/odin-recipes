Chocolate Milk

Description

Chocolate milk is a sweetened chocolate-flavored milk. It can be made by mixing chocolate syrup with milk. It can be purchased pre-mixed with milk or made at home by blending milk with cocoa powder and a sweetener, melted chocolate, chocolate syrup, or a pre-made powdered chocolate milk mix

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Nestle Quik

Steps

  1. Pour a big glass of milk
  2. Shovel a couple of scoops of Nestle Quik into the milk
  3. Stir well
  4. Enjoy
